Story

We first met in late 2019 at church, where we were paired up for an evangelical activity. Afterward, we exchanged contact details and kept in touch. Our conversations revealed we had a lot in common, and he discovered that I knew French, which he was eager to learn. What started as a simple exchange of knowledge quickly turned into regular chats, and before long, we became close friends, with him becoming an integral part of my daily life.
For his birthday in 2020, I gave him a gift as a gesture of friendship, and he was deeply touched. Then, during the lockdown, he sent me a thoughtful birthday gift, which made me feel incredibly special. After that, our bond grew even stronger, and by the time the lockdown ended, we were incredibly close. He knew practically everything about me, including my family, who adore him.
In 2022, we decided to take our relationship to the next level and got married. And now, here we are.
